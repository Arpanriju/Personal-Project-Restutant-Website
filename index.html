About Saffron Ember

Our Story

Established in 2003, Saffron Ember began as a culinary dream rooted in fire, flavor, and finesse. Nestled in the heart of the city, the restaurant was founded by Chef Anaya Deshmukh, a classically trained chef inspired by the bold spices of the East and the slow-cooked traditions of the West.

Every dish is prepared with love and attention to detail, using recipes passed down througn generations of Italian chefs.
